Relapse Is a Part of Recovery

It doesn’t mean failure. It means the healing process needs support. Learn to identify your warning signs and stay connected.

Common Triggers

  • Stress and emotional overwhelm
  • Isolation or loneliness
  • People, places, or media tied to past use
  • Celebrations or overconfidence in recovery

Prevention Tools

  • Make a safety plan and write it down
  • Keep a list of reasons for your recovery
  • Build a reliable support network
  • Have a "rescue box" or grounding tools ready

If You Slip Up

Talk to someone you trust. Don’t spiral into shame. Get back up. Keep going. One step at a time.

What We’re About

Speak Louder, Love Harder is a vibrant community built to uplift voices often unheard. Whether you're living with chronic illness, overcoming addiction, or healing from trauma, here you’ll find a welcoming space.

Who It's For

  • Individuals seeking support for mental health and addiction
  • People living with cystic fibrosis, cirrhosis, or other health challenges
  • Survivors of physical, mental, and sexual abuse
  • Lovers of pitbulls and advocates for foster children
  • Anyone wanting to share affirmations, grounding techniques, and positive stories

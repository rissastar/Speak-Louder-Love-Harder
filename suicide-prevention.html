You Matter. You Are Needed.

If you're having thoughts of suicide, please know: you're not alone. Many people feel overwhelmed, hopeless, or numb at times—but these feelings are survivable. There is help. There is hope. You are not a burden. Your story is not over.

Signs Someone May Be Struggling

  • Talking about wanting to die or not being here
  • Withdrawing from friends, family, or activities
  • Sudden calm after a period of deep sadness
  • Giving away possessions or saying goodbye

Check in with people you care about. A simple “Are you okay?” can mean the world.

If You're In Crisis

Reach out to someone safe. Try a grounding technique. Use the tools in this app. You don’t need to be “in a certain place” to deserve support. You are worthy exactly as you are.

  • Visual Spinner or Grounding Techniques can calm the body
  • Use the Hope Jar for reminders of your reasons to stay
  • Express yourself in the Emotion Box—even messy thoughts are welcome

Emergency Support

  • Canada: Talk Suicide Canada – 1-833-456-4566 or text 45645
  • USA: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call/text 988)
  • UK: Samaritans – 116 123 (free, 24/7)
  • Worldwide: findahelpline.com

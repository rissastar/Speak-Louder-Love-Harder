Recognizing Signs

Warning signs include talking about wanting to die, withdrawing from activities, or showing extreme mood swings.

Getting Help

Reach out to trusted people, professionals, or hotlines immediately if you or someone you know is struggling.

Resources & Hotlines

Call 988 (USA), 1-833-456-4566 (Canada), or visit crisis text lines and local emergency services.

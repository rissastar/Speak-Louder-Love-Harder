Mindfulness Practice

Mindfulness helps you stay present and reduce anxiety by focusing attention on the here and now.

  • Start with short breathing exercises.
  • Use guided meditation apps or audio tracks.
  • Practice mindful eating and walking.
  • Incorporate mindfulness into daily routines.

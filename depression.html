What is Depression?

Depression is a common but serious mood disorder affecting thoughts, feelings, and physical health. It can cause persistent sadness, loss of interest, and disrupt daily life.

Signs & Symptoms

  • Persistent sad or empty mood
  • Loss of interest or pleasure in usual activities
  • Fatigue or loss of energy
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Changes in appetite or sleep patterns
  • Feelings of worthlessness or guilt
  • Thoughts of death or suicide

Coping & Support

  • Reach out to trusted friends, family, or mental health professionals.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques.
  • Set small, manageable goals daily.
  • Maintain a balanced diet and sleep routine.
  • Avoid alcohol and recreational drugs.

Treatment Options

Treatments include psychotherapy (talk therapy), medications (antidepressants), lifestyle changes, or a combination. Work with healthcare providers to find the best approach.

Managing Chronic Pain

Pain management is an essential part of living with chronic conditions. Different strategies work for different people.

  • Medications as prescribed by healthcare providers.
  • Physical therapy and gentle exercise.
  • Mindfulness meditation and relaxation techniques.
  • Heat/cold therapy and assistive devices.

Symptom Tracking

Keeping track of symptoms can help identify triggers and evaluate treatment effectiveness.

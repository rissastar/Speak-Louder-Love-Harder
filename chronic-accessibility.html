Tools & Aids

There are many accessibility tools designed to help people with chronic conditions or disabilities live more independently and comfortably.

  • Screen readers and magnifiers for vision support.
  • Voice control and speech recognition software.
  • Mobility aids like canes, walkers, and wheelchairs.
  • Home modifications such as ramps and grab bars.

Cultivating Gratitude

Regularly recognizing what you are grateful for can boost your mood and increase resilience.

  • Keep a daily gratitude journal.
  • Reflect on positive moments each day.
  • Share appreciation with others.
  • Use a hope jar to collect uplifting notes and affirmations.

If you're struggling to cope, you're not alone. The following resources provide professional support:

  • National Bereavement Hotline (Canada): 1-866-264-5437
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Hospice & Palliative Care Services: Available through your local hospital

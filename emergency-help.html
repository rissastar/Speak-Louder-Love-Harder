You Are Not Alone

If you're in danger or need urgent support, please reach out. There are people ready to help, day or night.

24/7 Hotlines

  • Canada: Talk Suicide Canada – 1-833-456-4566
  • USA: National Suicide & Crisis Lifeline – 988
  • UK: Samaritans – 116 123
  • International: findahelpline.com

Immediate Steps

  • Go to a safe place or public space
  • Call emergency services (911, 112)
  • Text a trusted friend or adult

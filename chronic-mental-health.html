Mental Health Challenges

Mental health is a vital part of overall wellness, especially for those living with chronic conditions or disabilities. Depression, anxiety, and stress may be common but manageable with support.

Support & Resources

  • Seek therapy or counseling tailored to chronic illness.
  • Join peer support groups to share experiences and advice.
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques regularly.
  • Communicate openly with healthcare providers about mental health.

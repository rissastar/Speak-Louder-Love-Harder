Why Groups Help

You are not alone. Support groups provide empathy, accountability, and safe spaces to share and heal.

Common Groups

  • 📘 AA (Alcoholics Anonymous)
  • 📘 NA (Narcotics Anonymous)
  • 🌐 SMART Recovery (science-based)
  • 🌱 Refuge Recovery (Buddhist-based)
  • 🧠 Online peer groups (Reddit, Discord, forums)

Tips for Getting Started

  • Try different types to find your fit
  • You can stay anonymous
  • Start by listening—sharing is optional
  • Bring a notebook or a friend

Your Healing Circle

It doesn't have to be formal. A healing circle can be 2 friends who listen and don’t judge. That counts too.

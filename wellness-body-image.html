Building Positive Self-Esteem

Developing a healthy body image and self-esteem can improve your mental and emotional wellbeing.

  • Practice self-compassion and positive self-talk.
  • Limit exposure to unrealistic media standards.
  • Focus on what your body can do, not just how it looks.
  • Seek support if struggling with body image issues.

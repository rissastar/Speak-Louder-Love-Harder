Managing Stress Effectively

Stress is a natural response but chronic stress can harm health. Learning to manage stress helps improve wellbeing.

  • Practice deep breathing and progressive muscle relaxation.
  • Maintain a balanced schedule with breaks and hobbies.
  • Exercise regularly to reduce tension.
  • Seek social support from friends, family, or professionals.
